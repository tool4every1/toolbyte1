SEO Best Practices

Title Length

Keep title tags between 50-60 characters. Search engines may truncate longer titles in results.

Description Quality

Write compelling descriptions that include primary keywords and encourage clicks.

Mobile Optimization

Always include a viewport meta tag to ensure proper rendering on mobile devices.

Keyword Placement

Place important keywords near the beginning of your title and description tags.
