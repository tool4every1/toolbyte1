Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2025

At ToolByte1, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and tools.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited and tools used.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our services, including browser type and IP address.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze site traffic.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Develop new tools and features
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns
  • Prevent technical issues and security risks
  • Communicate with you about service updates

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using SSL technology
  • We regularly audit our security practices
  • We do not store any uploaded files longer than necessary to process your request

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data about user interactions. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking through your browser settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to object to certain processing activities

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
